This advisor helps you choose the right Microsoft platform based on your use case, data, and required level of control—from simple productivity to enterprise-grade AI solutions.

Microsoft 365 Copilot

Built-in AI for everyday work—helping users summarize, draft, and analyze content across Teams, Outlook, and Office apps.

Individual Productivity

Agent Builder

No-code tool for creating simple internal agents using Microsoft 365 content like documents, policies, and knowledge bases.

Team workflows

Copilot Studio

Low-code platform to build agents that automate workflows and connect to business systems like CRM, ERP, and ticketing tools.

Department solutions

Microsoft Foundry

Pro-code platform for building advanced, custom AI agents with full control over models, orchestration, and enterprise data.

Platform summary

Platform Best user persona Best use cases Best data

Recommended operating model

Use a single intake process and route requests through these four tracks:

  1. 1Default to Microsoft 365 Copilot for everyday productivity use cases.
  2. 2Use Agent Builder for fast no-code internal prototypes.
  3. 3Standardize workflow automation in Copilot Studio.
  4. 4Reserve Foundry for strategic custom and regulated workloads.
